Northumbria University

Northumbria is the largest University in the North East, with 33,000 students from over 125 countries. It is an expanding multicultural learning community, with excellent links assisting further and higher education, industry and commerce throughout the UK, Europe and beyond. It is renowned for the excellence of its teaching and preparing students for the world of work. Following pathway programmes are offered at ECube Global College in association with Northumbria University,

Programme: Bachelors in Management.(BBA)

Duration of Programme: TWO years in INDIA and ONE year with NORTHUMBRIA UNIVERSITY.

Nature of Programme: ECube Global has established an association with the University with their BA (Hons.) in Business with several top-up options.The Concept allows the students to enroll with ECube Global College for the first two years of the Programmes offering Edexcel HND’s (4 & 5) in Business Management.Having completed their HND’s the students will then be offered seats at the Northumbria University for the top-up BA (Hons.) degree in Business Management with Specialization Business and Accounting/ Business and Finance/ Business and HRM/ Business and Marketing/ Supply Chain Management/ International Business.The student also has option to complete his/her final year in India and UK. The student may even continue and complete his MBA/MSc. from the same University.

Programme:Computing and System Development(I.T Engineering)

Duration of Programme: Two years in INDIA and ONE year with NORTHUMBRIA UNIVERSITY.

Nature of programme: ECube Global has established an association with the University with their BA (Hons.)/ B.Sc / B.E in computing science/Engineering/I.T with several top-up options. The Concept allows the students to enroll with ECube Global College for the first two years of the Programmes offering Edexcel HND’s (4 & 5) in Computing and system development. Having completed their HND’s the students will then be offered seats at the Northumbria University for the top-up BA (Hons.)/ B.Sc / B.E in Computing Science/ IT Engineering/ Software Engineering/ Computer ad Gaming Development.
