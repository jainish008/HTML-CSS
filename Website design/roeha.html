Roehampton University

University of Roehampton sets itself apart from its competitors in many ways. In terms of its location, it is the only campus university in London and is ranked number one among London universities for environment and surroundings according to Times Higher Education.

The University is located on a beautiful parkland campus in south west London with historic buildings, lakes and on-site accommodation.

Roehampton is also ranked number one in London for employability. Their Employability and Entrepreneurship team are dedicated to supporting and guiding students on careers after graduation and part-time work while studying.

Roehampton has one of the most diverse student populations in the UK with representatives from over 130 countries around the world.

Programme: Bachelors in Management.(BBA)

Duration of Programme: TWO years in INDIA and ONE year with University of Roehampton.

Nature of Programme:: ECube Global has established an association with the University with their BA (Hons.) in Business with several top-up options. The Concept allows the students to enroll with ECube Global College for the first two years of the Programmes offering Edexcel HND’s (4 & 5) in Business Management. Having completed their HND’s the students will then be offered seats at the University of Roehampton for the top-up BA (Hons.) degree in Business Management with Specialization Business and Accounting/ Business and Finance/ Business and HRM/ Business and Marketing/ Supply Chain Management/ International Business. The student also has option to complete his/her final year in India and UK. The student may even continue and complete his MBA/MSc. from the same University.
