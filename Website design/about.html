Admission Process

Step1:

Collect the application kit from the college counter (Brochure + Form).

Step 2

Fill in the application form and submit it between 9 A.M. to 4 P.M during week days. With your application provide the following documents and photographs:-

  • A copy of your school leaving certificate or A-level certificate plus original for ratification.
  • A copy of your birth certificate plus original for verification.
  • Original mark sheet of Xth for Level 3 / Original mark sheet of Xth and XIIth for BBA.
  • Address Proof (Passport, Light bill, Telephone bill Ration Card, Adhaar Card, Driving License or any other governmental proof).
  • Pan of Parents or Guardian.
Step 3

If the admission is accepted, the following have to be submitted by the student within the stipulated timeframe to confirm the provisional admission.

  • Payment of Fees
  • Booklet of Rules, Regulations and Undertaking
  • Policies for Accommodation
  • A photocopy of your passport
  • Original mark sheet for Xth and XIIth*
  • 3 recent passport size photograph
